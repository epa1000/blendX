Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are available at Blandx Learning?
We offer a wide range of courses including Entrance Exams (JEE, NEET, CLAT), Government Exams (UPSC, SSC, Banking), Commerce (CA, CS), Science subjects, Technology courses, and Language learning programs.
How do I register for a course?
Registration is simple! Click on the "Register Now" button, fill in your details, choose your desired course, and complete the payment process. You'll get instant access to the course materials.
Are the courses available online?
Yes, all our courses are available online with 24/7 access. We also offer hybrid learning options with both online and offline classes for certain programs.
What is the fee structure?
Our fee structure varies depending on the course and duration. We offer flexible payment options including EMI plans. Please contact our support team for detailed fee information.
Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes, we provide placement assistance and career guidance for eligible courses. We have tie-ups with leading companies and organizations to help students secure good career opportunities.
